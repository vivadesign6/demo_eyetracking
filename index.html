Webcam Eye Tracking — Dissertation Prototype

This prototype demonstrates webcam-based eye tracking integrated into an e-commerce survey stimulus. Part of the dissertation: "Designing for Shopping Motivations: How Hedonic and Utilitarian Shoppers Differ in Visual Attention to Product Design Elements in E-Commerce Interfaces."

  • 1
    Calibration — click 9 dots while looking at each one (≈45 seconds)
  • 2
    Stimulus viewing — browse the product page naturally for up to 30 seconds
  • 3
    Results — see fixation metrics per AOI (TFD, FC, MFD, TTFF)
Requirements: Desktop or laptop with a working webcam. Chrome or Firefox recommended. No video is recorded — only gaze coordinates are stored locally.
